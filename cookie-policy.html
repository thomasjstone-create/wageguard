Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Wage Guard uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and understand website usage.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and provide a smoother browsing experience.

How we use cookies

  • Essential cookies for site performance and security.
  • Analytics cookies to understand how visitors use the website.
  • Preference cookies to remember language or display settings when present.

Third-party cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies, such as analytics tools. These providers may have their own privacy and cookie policies.

Managing cookies

You can change your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect how the site works.
