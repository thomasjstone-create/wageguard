Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data Wage Guard collects and how we use it when you request a quote or explore our services.

Information we collect

  • Contact information such as name, email address and phone number.
  • Employment and income details required to provide an income protection quote.
  • Device and browsing data to improve the website experience.

How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Provide insurance quotes and compare cover options.
  • Contact you to discuss your requirements and help complete your application.
  • Maintain and improve our services and website.

Legal basis

When you request a quote, we process your personal data on the basis of your consent and to fulfil our contract with you. We also process data to comply with legal and regulatory obligations where required.

Sharing your data

We may share your information with:

  • FCA authorised insurers and comparison partners to provide quotes.
  • Service providers who support our website, security and communications.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect your data. Access is limited to authorised personnel, and we regularly review our systems to help keep your information secure.

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies to make the website work properly and to analyse traffic. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at help@wageguard.co.uk.
